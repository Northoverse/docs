## Our Planned Ventures

We have a few companies planned for the future.

<Columns cols={1}>
  <Card title="Cheroann - Remote Work Company" icon="chart-network" iconType="duotone" href="/cheroann" cta="YEAR2">
    A company dedicated to creating a remote work marketplace, so that as the startup portfolio grows and unemployment in local areas disappears then we should keep focusing on expanding and growing the startups, they can find employees faster with this startup, even if those employees are in other countries they can help and this will also help spreading our startups internationally even if its just collaboration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ARM  - Product Manufacturing Company" icon="pretzel" iconType="duotone" href="/arm" cta="YEAR3">
    A company dedicated to transforming visionary ideas into tangible, market-ready products, so that as a startup's brand gains traction and demand for their unique product grows, they have a reliable manufacturing partner to scale with them. This allows inventors and founders to stop grappling with complex supply chains and production logistics and focus entirely on design, marketing, and sales.

    With ARM handling the entire manufacturing lifecycle, even a solo entrepreneur can produce goods with the efficiency and quality of a large corporation, and this robust production capability will also pave the way for seamless international distribution and global brand growth when they are ready.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Food Truck Network - A Franchise Concept-Based Food Truck Company" icon="meat" iconType="duotone" href="/foodtrucknetwork" cta="YEAR3">
    A company dedicated to democratizing entrepreneurship in the food industry by creating a powerful, recognizable network of food truck franchises, so that as culinary trends evolve and consumer demand for convenient, high-quality street food grows, aspiring restaurateurs can launch their own business with the support of a major brand. This allows a franchisee to start with a single truck or a prime location space, bypassing the immense costs and risks of building a concept from scratch.

    With the Food Truck Network providing the brand, systems, and supply chain, even a first-time owner can operate with the marketing power and operational efficiency of a chain, and this established foundation will also enable successful owners to rapidly scale by adding more trucks or territories within the network.
  </Card>
  <Card title="BitTm Exchange - A Crypto ATM Company" icon="bitcoin-sign" iconType="duotone" href="/bittm-exchange" cta="YEAR3">
    A company dedicated to bridging the gap between traditional finance and the digital currency economy by deploying a network of accessible Crypto ATMs, so that as cryptocurrency adoption grows and the demand for easy, tangible entry points increases, local communities have a trusted and convenient way to buy and sell Bitcoin and other major cryptocurrencies. This allows individuals to interact with the digital asset world as easily as using a traditional bank ATM, without the complexity of online exchanges.

    With BitTm Exchange managing the regulatory compliance, technology, and security, we can rapidly scale a network of kiosks in high-traffic locations, and this physical presence will also help build mainstream trust and drive financial inclusion for the next wave of crypto users.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Particle Shredder - A 3D Printing Filament Manufacturing Company" icon="chart-scatter-3d" iconType="duotone" href="/particle-shredder" cta="YEAR4">
    A company dedicated to closing the loop on plastic waste by transforming local discarded materials into premium 3D printing filament, so that as environmental concerns grow and the demand for sustainable manufacturing materials increases, communities and creators have a direct way to contribute to a circular economy. This allows individuals to exchange eligible plastics for credits, diverting waste from landfills while empowering makers with affordable, eco-conscious supplies.

    With Particle Shredder establishing this recycling-to-production pipeline, we not only build a loyal customer base but also secure a cost-effective, locally-sourced raw material stream. This foundational model will allow us to first supply individual buyers and 3D printing farms, and then vertically integrate by using our own filament to manufacture proprietary products, creating a fully sustainable, local manufacturing ecosystem.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Particle Farm - A 3D Printing Farm Company" icon="shoe-prints" iconType="duotone" href="/particle-farm" cta="YEAR4">
    A company dedicated to leveraging our locally sourced, recycled filament to provide high-volume 3D printing services, so that as businesses and creators seek agile and sustainable manufacturing solutions, they have a partner that can handle everything from prototype runs to full-scale production. This allows us to directly utilize the premium filament from Particle Shredder, creating a closed-loop, eco-friendly manufacturing process that validates our material's quality and durability under real-world demand.

    With Particle Farm managing a fleet of printers, we can generate revenue through client contracts, fulfill our own product lines, and actively promote the brand by producing free items for marketing campaigns and non-profit giveaways, turning our printing capacity into both a profit center and a powerful tool for community engagement and brand advocacy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="DPN - Delivery Pod Network" icon="pot-food" iconType="duotone" href="/DPN" cta="YEAR4">
    A company dedicated to redefining logistics by building a subterranean, magnetic-levitation network for urban delivery, so that as e-commerce demand intensifies and above-ground traffic becomes increasingly gridlocked, cities and residents have a high-speed, hyper-efficient alternative for last-mile and last-inch delivery.

    This system transports standardized pods in train-like sequences to neighborhood stations, bypassing traditional roadways entirely.

    By connecting directly to apartment infrastructures, we can automate the final leg of the journey, moving packages from the central drop point directly to the customer's doorstep or a designated in-unit receptacle, much like a modern trash chute.

    This will fundamentally change the speed, cost, and reliability of urban delivery, alleviating traffic congestion and creating a seamless, 24/7 logistical backbone for the cities of tomorrow.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Particle Factory - A drone manufacturing company" icon="drone" iconType="duotone" href="/particle-factory" cta="YEAR5">
    A company dedicated to applying our advanced manufacturing expertise to the defense and security sector by developing and producing next-generation unmanned systems, so that as global and domestic security challenges evolve and the demand for agile, intelligent surveillance and response tools grows, our defense and law enforcement partners have a reliable, technologically superior supplier. This strategic pivot allows us to leverage our proven capabilities in precision fabrication and supply chain management to serve a critical national security need.

    With Particle Factory focusing initially on the specific, pressing demands of island security—such as vast maritime patrol and rapid response over difficult terrain—we will establish a proven track record and a robust product line, creating a solid foundation to compete for larger defense orders and become a key partner in modern security infrastructure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Caffeine - A space agency" icon="shuttle-space" iconType="duotone" href="/caffeine" cta="YEAR5">
    A company dedicated to securing humanity's future as a multi-planetary species by developing advanced, autonomous systems for planetary security and orbital operations, so that as our presence in space expands and the strategic importance of celestial resources grows, nations and corporations have a partner capable of ensuring safety and sustainability beyond Earth's atmosphere.

    This mission builds upon our legacy of precision manufacturing and unmanned systems, pushing the boundaries of technology to create space-capable drones for orbital maintenance, asteroid monitoring, and planetary reconnaissance.

    With Caffeine focusing initially on critical infrastructure like securing lunar and Martian outposts for relay stations to create a proven slingshot highway for fast automated payload delivery in space and monitoring orbital traffic, we will establish the essential frameworks for interplanetary security and resource management, enabling a safe and accelerated era of space expansion for all.
  </Card>
