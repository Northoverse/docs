This allows individuals to interact with the digital asset world as easily as using a traditional bank ATM, without the complexity of online exchanges.

    With BitTm Exchange managing the regulatory compliance, technology, and security, we can rapidly scale a network of kiosks in high-traffic locations, and this physical presence will also help build mainstream trust and drive financial inclusion for the next wave of crypto users.
