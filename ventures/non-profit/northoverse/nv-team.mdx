# üìä Bi-Weekly Reporting Flow

This table illustrates the chain of command for the **Bi-Weekly Report** submitted to the Founder.

| Level | Role / Tier | Responsibilities | Reports To |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 4** | **Interns, Volunteers & Assistants** | Daily execution of tasks. Reports status every Friday. | Department Manager |
| **Tier 3** | **Managers & Leads** | *Examples: Program Manager, HR Manager, Media Lead.* <br /> Aggregates intern data into Dept Report. | **The C-Suite** (Monday) |
| **Tier 2** | **C-Suite (COO, CIO, CCO)** | Filters noise, highlights KPIs & Blockers. Creates Dashboard. | **Founder** (Bi-Weekly) |
| **Tier 1** | **Founder** | Reviewing strategic progress and unblocking high-level issues. | Stakeholders / Board |
