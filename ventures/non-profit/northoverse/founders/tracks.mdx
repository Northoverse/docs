# üõë House Rules (The Code)

_Northoverse is a community built on trust. Violation of these rules is grounds for immediate dismissal from the cohort._

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="1. The 'Safe Space' Rule" icon="shield-heart" iconType="solid">

  </Card>
  <Card title="2. The 'Give First' Rule" icon="hand-holding-heart" iconType="solid">

# üéì Graduation & Network Status

_Graduation is just the beginning. We distinguish between those who learned (Fellows) and those who built (Alumni)._

### Level 1: Northoverse Fellows (Pre-Alumni)

_Awarded to anyone who successfully completes the educational requirements of a track (80% attendance + homework) but has NOT yet launched._

1. **The Certificate:** A digital credential verifying your training completion.
2. **The Refund:** Your commitment fee is returned in full.
3. **Community Access:** You retain access to the `#general` Slack channels to stay in the loop.
4. **Audit Rights:** You can sit in on future workshops to refresh your skills.

### Level 2: Portfolio Alumni (Full Status)

**_Awarded only when you have incorporated your business and formally joined the Northoverse Portfolio._**

1. **The Directory Listing:** Your company is featured on the Northoverse website for investors to see.
2. **Investor Pipelines:** Direct introductions to our network of Angels and VCs.
3. **Nessty & Odoo Perks:** Continued free access to our enterprise software licenses for 12 months.
4. **EIR Access:** Priority booking for 1-on-1 strategy sessions with our Entrepreneurs-in-Residence.
