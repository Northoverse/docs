# üìñ The Case Library (University Edition)

Unlike High School cases (which have a clear "right" answer), College cases are about **trade-offs**.

### üë®‚Äçüè´ Instructor's Guide: The "3-Step Debate"
*How to run these sessions in a Student Union or Classroom.*

1.  **The Cold Call:** Ask one student to summarize the "Core Conflict" in 60 seconds.
2.  **The Vote:** Ask the room: "Who would Fire the employee? Who would Keep them?" (Force a binary choice).
3.  **The Devil's Advocate:** Challenge the majority opinion with new facts (e.g., "What if I told you firing them loses us 40% revenue?").
