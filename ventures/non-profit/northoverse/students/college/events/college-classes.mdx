# üéì The Campus Curriculum

University teaches theory. We teach execution. These modules are designed to bridge the gap between your degree and your first paycheck (or funding check).

## üöÄ Track 1: The Venture Founder (Launchpad I)
*Phase 1: From Idea to "MVP" (Minimum Viable Product).*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Module 1: The Validation Sprint">
    **Focus:** Customer Discovery & The "Mom Test".
    * **Assignment:** Conduct 20 B2B or 50 B2C user interviews.
    * **Submission:** A "Pain Point Analysis" Matrix mapping frequency vs. intensity of the problem.
    * **Deadline:** Week 1, Friday @ 11:59 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 2: Advanced Business Modeling">
    **Focus:** Unit Economics & CAC/LTV.
    * **Assignment:** Build a spreadsheet modeling Customer Acquisition Cost vs. Lifetime Value.
    * **Submission:** `.XLS` Model showing a clear path to profitability.
    * **Deadline:** Week 2, Friday @ 11:59 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 3: No-Code MVP Architecture">
    **Focus:** Database Schema & Logic.
    * **Assignment:** Map out the User Flow and Data Relationships (Users -> Orders -> Items).
    * **Submission:** Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) + Bubble/Glide Prototype Link.
    * **Deadline:** Week 3, Friday @ 11:59 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 4: GTM (Go-to-Market) Strategy">
    **Focus:** The First 100 Customers.
    * **Assignment:** Execute a "Cold Outreach" campaign using Apollo or LinkedIn Sales Nav.
    * **Submission:** CSV Export of 100 leads with Status (Open, Clicked, Replied).
    * **Deadline:** Week 4, Friday @ 11:59 PM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## üöÄ Track 1: The Scale-Up Founder (Launchpad II)
*Phase 2: From MVP to "Fundable Company".*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Module 5: Fundraising & Term Sheets">
    **Focus:** Venture Capital Literacy.
    * **Assignment:** Analyze a mock "Safe Note" and mark up a "Series A Term Sheet" with redlines.
    * **Submission:** 12-Slide Pre-Seed Pitch Deck + Cap Table Model.
    * **Deadline:** Week 6, Friday @ 11:59 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 6: Incorporation & IP Defense">
    **Focus:** Legal Structure & Moats.
    * **Assignment:** Draft a "Founder Agreement" with 4-year vesting and 1-year cliff.
    * **Submission:** Incorporation Checklist & Trademark Search Report.
    * **Deadline:** Week 7, Friday @ 11:59 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 7: Hiring & Culture OS">
    **Focus:** HR & Leadership.
    * **Assignment:** Write a Job Description (JD) and design a "First 30 Days" onboarding plan.
    * **Submission:** Performance Review Template + Cultural Values Manifesto.
    * **Deadline:** Week 8, Friday @ 11:59 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 8: The Exit Strategy">
    **Focus:** M&A and IPOs.
    * **Assignment:** Identify 5 potential acquirers and write a "Strategic Rationale" for why they would buy you.
    * **Submission:** 1-Page M&A Teaser.
    * **Deadline:** Week 9, Friday @ 11:59 PM.
  </Step>
</Steps>
