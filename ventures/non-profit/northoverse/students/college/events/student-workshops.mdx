# üìù Booking Protocol (For Educators)

_How to bring Northoverse to your school._

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Choose Your Format">
    Decide if you want a **Virtual** (Zoom) session or an **In-Person** visit (Charlottetown area only).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Check Eligibility">
    **Public Schools:** Workshops are **100% Free** (Subsidized by our corporate partners). **Universities:** Usually free if hosted by a Student Union or Faculty.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Submit Request">
    Email `northoverse@gmail.com` with:

    - School Name
    - Class Size (e.g., 25 students)
    - Preferred Date/Time
    - Workshop Topic
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: The Prep Packet">
    We send you a 1-page PDF to distribute to students beforehand (usually just asking them to bring a laptop or download a specific free tool).
  </Step>
</Steps>
