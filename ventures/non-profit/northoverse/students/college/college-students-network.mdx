### üëë Role: Campus Captain
**Commitment:** 5 Hours/Week.
**Responsibility:** Host 1 monthly mixer, manage the campus Slack channel, and scout talent.
**Compensation:**
* $500/Semester Stipend.
* "Guaranteed Interview" for Summer Internships.
* Exclusive "Captain" Hoodie & Northoverse Swag Pack.
* Letter of Recommendation from the CEO.

### üïµÔ∏è Role: The Scout
**Commitment:** Flexible.
**Responsibility:** Identify students building cool things in their dorms and introduce them to us.
**Compensation:**
* $50 "Bounty" for every student you refer who gets accepted into the Incubator.
* Free Coffee for life at the Northoverse Hub.

## üíº Internship & Co-Op Pipelines
*Don't fetch coffee. Build companies. We are a registered employer with UPEI Co-op and SkillsPEI.*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Choose Your Track">
    **Tech Stream:** Build features for the *Nessty-AI* platform (React/Python).
    **Marketing Stream:** Run social campaigns for our 50+ portfolio startups.
    **Ops Stream:** Manage logistics for Demo Days at The Guild.
    **Design Stream:** Create brand assets and UI kits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: The 'No-Resume' Application">
    We don't care about your GPA. We care about what you've built.
    * **Submission:** Send us a link to a project (GitHub, Portfolio, Blog, or a Business Plan).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: The 'Vibe Check' Interview">
    A 15-minute chat to see if you fit our culture of "Move Fast and Break Things."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Placement">
    You are either placed internally at Northoverse HQ or "loaned out" to one of our high-growth portfolio startups.
  </Step>
</Steps>
