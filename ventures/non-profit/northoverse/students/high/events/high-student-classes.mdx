## üíª Track 1: The Digital Founder (12-Module Journey)

_The core curriculum. A complete semester-long journey from "Idea" to "Launch."_

<Steps>
  <Step title="Module 1: The Idea Validator">
    **Focus:** The "Mom Test" Methodology. Stop guessing. Learn how to interview strangers to see if your idea is actually good before spending a dime.

    - **Submission:** 5 Recorded Audio Interviews (min. 2 mins each).
    - **Deadline:** Week 1, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 2: The One-Page Plan">
    **Focus:** The Lean Canvas. Forget 50-page business plans. We map your entire business strategy on a single sheet of paper.

    - **Submission:** Completed Lean Canvas PDF (9 Blocks filled).
    - **Deadline:** Week 2, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 3: Unit Economics">
    **Focus:** "Lemonade Stand" Math. Understanding COGS (Cost of Goods Sold), Profit Margins, and Break-Even points.

    - **Submission:** "Breakeven Calculator" Spreadsheet (.XLS).
    - **Deadline:** Week 3, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 4: Branding on a Budget">
    **Focus:** DIY Design. Using color psychology and free tools to create a logo and brand voice that looks expensive.

    - **Submission:** Brand Style Guide (Logo, Hex Codes, Font Pairing).
    - **Deadline:** Week 4, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 5: The No-Code MVP">
    **Focus:** Prototyping. Building a "Minimum Viable Product" (a working website or app) without writing code.

    - **Submission:** Live Link to Landing Page (Carrd/Wix) or Prototype.
    - **Deadline:** Week 6, Friday @ 5:00 PM (2-Week Sprint).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 6: Legal Reality Check">
    **Focus:** Teen Law. Permits, taxes, and contracts. Can a minor sign a contract? Do you need insurance?

    - **Submission:** "Legal Risk Assessment" Document (1 Page).
    - **Deadline:** Week 7, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 7: The Sales Funnel">
    **Focus:** Customer Acquisition. Moving people from "Stranger" to "Lead" to "Customer" using the AIDA model.

    - **Submission:** Sales Script Draft (Cold Email or DM Script).
    - **Deadline:** Week 8, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 8: Digital Marketing">
    **Focus:** Content Strategy. How to use TikTok, Instagram, and SEO for business (organic growth), not just for fun.

    - **Submission:** 30-Day Content Calendar & 3 Mockup Posts.
    - **Deadline:** Week 9, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 9: Operations & Systems">
    **Focus:** Workflow. Setting up systems so you aren't working 24/7. Introduction to SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures).

    - **Submission:** One detailed "SOP" Document for a core task.
    - **Deadline:** Week 10, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 10: Team Dynamics">
    **Focus:** Leadership. How to find a co-founder, split equity fairly, and handle conflict without ruining friendships.

    - **Submission:** "Founder Agreement" Terms Sheet.
    - **Deadline:** Week 11, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 11: The Perfect Pitch">
    **Focus:** Storytelling. Building a 10-slide pitch deck that investors (or competition judges) will love.

    - **Submission:** Final Pitch Deck (Google Slides) + Script.
    - **Deadline:** Week 12, Wednesday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 12: Launch Day">
    **Focus:** Go-to-Market. Executing a launch event, gathering initial feedback, and pivoting based on data.

    - **Submission:** "Post-Mortem" Report (What worked, what failed).
    - **Deadline:** Week 13, Friday @ 5:00 PM.
  </Step>
</Steps>
