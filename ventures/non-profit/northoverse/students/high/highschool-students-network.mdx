## üéñÔ∏è Student Leadership Roles

### 1. The "Northoverse Captain" (Ambassador)

_Be the leader of entrepreneurship at your school._

- **Role:** You run the weekly "Venture Club" meetings (we provide the pizza and slides). You are the bridge between your Principal and Northoverse.
- **The "University" Perk:** Being a Captain looks **incredible** on university applications. We write premium Reference Letters for your admissions.
- **The Swag:** Exclusive "Captain" Hoodie and Northoverse laptop stickers.

### 2. The "Junior Scout"

_Find the hidden talent._

- **Role:** Identify students who are building cool things (art, code, inventions) in their basements and bring them to Northoverse.
- **Reward:** \$50 bounty for every student you scout who gets accepted into the **Digital Founder Track**.
