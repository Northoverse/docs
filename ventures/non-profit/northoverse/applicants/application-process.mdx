# üó∫Ô∏è The General Founder Journey
*Before you enter a specific track, most applicants go through this standard intake funnel to ensure they are matched with the right resources.*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Phase 1: Online Application">
    **Status:** Rolling Basis
    Submit the Intake Form on the specific program page.
    * **Requirement:** 200-word description of your idea & your primary goal.
    * **Note:** No formal business plan is required at this stage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Phase 2: Screening & Eligibility">
    **Timeline:** 3‚Äì5 Business Days
    Our internal team reviews your submission against our [Eligibility Criteria](/eligibility).
    * **Outcome:** Interview Invite, Deferral, or Referral to partner agency.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Phase 3: The 'Vibe Check' Interview">
    **Format:** 20-Minute Zoom Call
    A conversation to assess coachability and time commitment.
    * **For ETP:** We check willingness to learn.
    * **For Incubation:** We discuss business viability.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Phase 4: Acceptance & Onboarding">
    **Action:** Sign Code of Conduct
    You receive access to Slack, the Google Classroom portal, and the event calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## ü™∂ Indigenous Entrepreneur Pathway
*A flexible, community-centered timeline designed to respect cultural protocols and individual pacing.*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: The Community Intake">
    **Action:** Submit "Heritage & Idea" Form.
    A conversation about who you are, where you are from, and what you want to build.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Circle Welcome">
    **Action:** Welcome Ceremony.
    Before business planning begins, you are welcomed by the Program Lead and an Elder/Mentor to discuss your vision.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Milestone A ‚Äî Story & Vision">
    **Focus:** Cultural Branding.
    Work with mentors to define your business identity and values.
    *Output:* A Brand Story Document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Milestone B ‚Äî Structure & Grants">
    **Focus:** Logistics & Funding.
    We connect you with specific Indigenous business grants (e.g., CCAB) and help register the business.
    *Output:* Grant Applications Submitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Graduation Feast">
    **Action:** Community Showcase.
    Present your business to the community and move to Alumni status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## üë©‚Äçüíº Women Founders Circle
*A structured 8-week cohort focused on leadership, funding, and scaling.*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Competitive Application">
    **Requirement:** Validation Data.
    We review revenue, user growth, or prototype status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Peer Panel Interview">
    **Format:** 30-Minute Zoom.
    Meet with alumni to assess peer-fit and coachability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Weeks 1-4 (Leadership)">
    **Focus:** The Internal Game.
    Workshops on negotiation, imposter syndrome, and hiring.
    *Output:* 3-Year Strategic Growth Plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Weeks 5-8 (Funding)">
    **Focus:** The External Pitch.
    Workshops on cap tables, valuation, and investor relations.
    *Output:* Polished Investor Deck.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Pitch Night">
    **Finale:** Live Presentation.
    Pitch to a room of angel investors and community leaders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## üõ†Ô∏è Entrepreneurial Training Program (ETP)
*A fast-paced "Zero-to-One" bootcamp for beginners.*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Registration">
    **Action:** Instant Sign-Up.
    Pay the refundable commitment fee to secure your seat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Weekend 1 ‚Äî Ideation">
    **Focus:** Problem Solving.
    Enter with a vague idea, leave with 3 concrete business concepts.
    *Activity:* "Crazy 8s" Brainstorming.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Weekend 2 ‚Äî Validation">
    **Focus:** Customer Discovery.
    Get out of the building and interview 10 potential customers.
    *Output:* Customer Persona Profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Weekend 3 ‚Äî Legal/Finance">
    **Focus:** The "Boring" Stuff.
    Lawyer and Accountant guest speakers explain taxes and incorporation.
    *Output:* 12-Month Cash Flow Sheet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Weekend 4 ‚Äî Launch">
    **Focus:** The First Sale.
    Set up a landing page and try to get your first dollar.
    *Reward:* Certificate & Fee Refund.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## üìÑ Business Plan Assistance
*A consulting-style track for founders who need documents done.*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: The 'Raw Data' Dump">
    **Action:** Upload Materials.
    Send us your sketches, spreadsheets, and notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Diagnostic Call">
    **Format:** 1-on-1 Strategy Session.
    We identify gaps in your marketing or financial logic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Writing Sprint (Async)">
    **Duration:** 2 Weeks.
    You rewrite sections based on feedback; we edit and format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Financial Stress Test">
    **Action:** Spreadsheet Review.
    We stress-test your financial model against bank loan standards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Final Handover">
    **Output:** The "Bank-Ready" PDF.
    Receive a professional Business Plan and Pitch Deck.
  </Step>
</Steps>
