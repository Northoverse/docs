# ðŸŽ“ Digital Classrooms (For Schools)
Northoverse provides these **free, deployable curriculum packs** for High Schools and Colleges. Designed for Google Classroom integration, these modules help educators bring entrepreneurship into the classroom.

<Columns cols={2}>

  <Card title="Module 1: The 'Side Hustle' Sprint" icon="1" iconType="regular" cta="DOWNLOAD">
    **Target:** High School (Grades 10-12)
    A 2-week rapid ideation course where students identify a problem in their local community and design a low-cost solution.
    
    * **Curriculum:** Problem spotting, empathy mapping, and "lemonade stand" economics.
    * **Output:** Students create a 1-page "Hustle Plan."
  </Card>

  <Card title="Module 2: Zero-Budget Brand Builder" icon="2" iconType="regular" cta="DOWNLOAD">
    **Target:** High School & Early College
    A digital marketing crash course focusing on organic growth. Students learn how to build a brand using only free tools (Canva, Social Media, Carrd).
    
    * **Curriculum:** Color psychology, logo design basics, and writing copy that sells.
    * **Output:** A full brand style guide and mock Instagram launch grid.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Module 3: The No-Code MVP" icon="3" iconType="regular" cta="DOWNLOAD">
    **Target:** College / University
    How to build a tech product without writing code. This module teaches students to use tools like Notion, Airtable, and Zapier to build functional prototypes.
    
    * **Curriculum:** Wireframing, database basics, and automation logic.
    * **Output:** A working web app or landing page prototype.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Module 4: Impact Innovators (Social Enterprise)" icon="4" iconType="regular" cta="DOWNLOAD">
    **Target:** High School / Student Unions
    Focuses on the NGO and Non-Profit model. Students learn the difference between "Profit" and "Purpose" and how to balance both.
    
    * **Curriculum:** The UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), fundraising ethics, and volunteer management.
    * **Output:** A pitch deck for a social cause campaign.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Module 5: Pitch Perfect" icon="5" iconType="regular" cta="DOWNLOAD">
    **Target:** All Levels
    A public speaking and persuasion module designed to help introverted students find their voice. 
    
    * **Curriculum:** Storytelling arcs, body language, and slide design.
    * **Output:** Students record and submit a 60-second "Elevator Pitch" video.
  </Card>

</Columns>
