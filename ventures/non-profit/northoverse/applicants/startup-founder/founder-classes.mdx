## üèõÔ∏è Track 1: The CEO Operating System
*A complete 16-module executive transformation. From "Builder" to "Leader."*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Module 1: Board Management Dynamics">
    **Phase 1: Governance.** Managing your boss (The Board).
    * **Submission:** A **Quarterly Board Deck** (15 slides) including CEO Report, KPIs, and The "Ask".
    * **Deadline:** Week 1, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 2: Investor Relations (IR)">
    **Phase 1: Governance.** The Narrative Arc.
    * **Submission:** A **Monthly Investor Update** template (Good, Bad, Ugly format).
    * **Deadline:** Week 2, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 3: Advanced Cap Table Engineering">
    **Phase 1: Governance.** Equity Strategy.
    * **Submission:** A dynamic **Carta/Excel Model** simulating a $15M Series A round with Pro-Rata calculations.
    * **Deadline:** Week 3, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 4: The Series A Data Room">
    **Phase 1: Governance.** Due Diligence.
    * **Submission:** A structured **Data Room Index** (Folder structure & file list).
    * **Deadline:** Week 4, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 5: Executive Hiring (The 'Bar Raiser')">
    **Phase 2: People.** Hiring people smarter than you.
    * **Submission:** An **Executive Offer Letter** with vesting schedules, cliffs, and acceleration clauses.
    * **Deadline:** Week 5, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 6: Organizational Design">
    **Phase 2: People.** Structure & Matrix Management.
    * **Submission:** An **Org Chart Projection** (Current vs. 12 Months out).
    * **Deadline:** Week 6, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 7: Compensation Strategy">
    **Phase 2: People.** Global Pay & Banding.
    * **Submission:** A **Salary Band Spreadsheet** for levels L1 (Junior) to L6 (VP).
    * **Deadline:** Week 7, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 8: Performance OS (OKRs)">
    **Phase 2: People.** Alignment.
    * **Submission:** A company-wide **OKR Dashboard** for Q3.
    * **Deadline:** Week 8, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 9: Advanced FP&A">
    **Phase 3: Finance.** Unit Economics at Scale.
    * **Submission:** A **24-Month Operating Budget** with Base, Bull, and Bear scenarios.
    * **Deadline:** Week 9, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 10: Global Expansion Strategy">
    **Phase 3: Finance.** New Markets.
    * **Submission:** A **Market Entry Go-to-Market Plan** for one new country (US/EMEA).
    * **Deadline:** Week 10, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 11: IP & Legal Moats">
    **Phase 3: Finance.** Defensibility.
    * **Submission:** An **IP Audit Report** identifying risks in your code/brand.
    * **Deadline:** Week 11, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 12: Enterprise Compliance (SOC2)">
    **Phase 3: Finance.** Selling Up-Market.
    * **Submission:** A **Security Policy** draft for the employee handbook.
    * **Deadline:** Week 12, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 13: M&A Strategy (Buy Side)">
    **Phase 4: Exit.** Inorganic Growth.
    * **Submission:** A **Target List** of 5 potential companies you could buy.
    * **Deadline:** Week 13, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 14: The Exit (Sell Side)">
    **Phase 4: Exit.** Getting Bought.
    * **Submission:** A 1-Page **"Teaser" Document** (Blind profile of your company).
    * **Deadline:** Week 14, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 15: Crisis Leadership">
    **Phase 4: Exit.** Wartime CEO.
    * **Submission:** A **Crisis Communication Plan** (Internal email + Public statement).
    * **Deadline:** Week 15, Friday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Module 16: Founder Psychology">
    **Phase 4: Exit.** Sustainability.
    * **Submission:** A personal **"360 Review"** analysis and 12-month self-development plan.
    * **Deadline:** Week 16, Friday.
  </Step>
</Steps>
