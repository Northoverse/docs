    - **Status:** You do _not_ need an incorporated business. You just need an idea.
    - **Availability:** Must be available for weekend bootcamps (Sat/Sun) for 4 consecutive weeks.
    - **Output:** willingness to complete homework assignments (Market Research, Financial Projections).

    - **Data:** You must bring your own raw data (competitor research, pricing ideas). We help you structure it, we don't invent it for you.
    - **Timeline:** For founders planning to submit funding applications within the next 3-6 months.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Is there a cost?" icon="dollar-sign" iconType="solid">
    **Most programs are fully subsidized** by our partners and grants.

    - **Digital Classrooms:** 100% Free.
    - **Indigenous & Women's Tracks:** May have full Scholarships available or May have a small commitment fee (refundable upon completion) to ensure attendance.
    - **ETP:** May have a small commitment fee (refundable upon completion) to ensure attendance.
  </Card>
