# ‚öñÔ∏è How We Evaluate

At Northoverse, we do not just look for "unicorn" business ideas; we look for founders with the resilience, ethics, and community spirit to build them.

To ensure fairness across our **70/30 Quota** (General Public vs. Student Grads), our Selection Committee uses a specific framework that values **Life Experience** just as much as **Technical Skill**.



---

## üß¨ The "Northoverse DNA" (Universal Traits)
*Regardless of the track, every applicant is scored on these four core human qualities. If you score low here, a great business idea will not save the application.*

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="1. Coachability" icon="ear-listen" iconType="solid" color="#27AE60">
    **The Metric:** Can you listen without getting defensive?
    * **Green Flag:** "That's a great point, I hadn't thought of that perspective."
    * **Red Flag:** "No, you don't understand, the customers are wrong."
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Resilience (Grit)" icon="mountain" iconType="solid" color="#E67E22">
    **The Metric:** Evidence of overcoming adversity.
    * **Green Flag:** Sharing a specific story of a past failure and the lesson learned.
    * **Red Flag:** Blaming external forces (economy, partners) for every past failure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Community Mindset" icon="users-rays" iconType="solid" color="#3498DB">
    **The Metric:** Willingness to "Give First."
    * **Green Flag:** History of volunteering or mentoring others.
    * **Red Flag:** Viewing other cohort members solely as competitors.
  </Card>
  <Card title="4. Resourcefulness" icon="toolbox" iconType="solid" color="#8E44AD">
    **The Metric:** Doing a lot with a little.
    * **Green Flag:** "I used a free Google Form to validate this."
    * **Red Flag:** "I can't start until I get a $50,000 grant."
  </Card>
</Columns>

## üéØ Track-Specific Scorecards

### üîì For Track 1 (Active Programs)
*Focus: Potential, Commitment & "Why Now". We bet on the Person.*

| Criteria | Weight | Specific Evidence We Look For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Commitment** | **40%** | Will you show up? For ETP, we need weekend availability. For Women Founders, we need evening consistency. |
| **Problem Clarity** | **40%** | Do you understand *who* is in pain and *why* current solutions fail? We don't care about your solution yet; we care about the problem. |
| **Why Now?** | **20%** | Why are you the right person to solve this? (e.g., "I worked in this industry for 10 years" or "I just graduated with this specific degree"). |

### üîí For Future Tracks (Growth & Deep Tech)
*Focus: Metrics, Traction & IP. We bet on the Data.*

| Criteria | Weight | Specific Evidence We Look For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Traction** | **50%** | **Proof of Life:** Revenue, active users, LOIs (Letters of Intent), or a functioning MVP. |
| **Scalability** | **30%** | Can this grow beyond PEI? Is the TAM (Total Addressable Market) large enough to support venture scale? |
| **Team/IP** | **20%** | Do you own the Intellectual Property? Do you have the technical skills in-house to build it? |

## üìä The Quota System (70/30)

We balance our cohorts to ensure diversity of thought.

| Applicant Type | Allocation | Evaluation Bias |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **General Public** | **70% of Seats** | **Bias: Life Experience.**<br/>We look for domain expertise (e.g., a nurse building a health app) and community connection. |
| **Student Grads** | **30% of Seats** | **Bias: Technical Skill.**<br/>We look for fresh ideas and modern tech stacks. Applicants must have completed the **College Graduate Checklist**. |

> **Note:** If the 30% Student Quota is not filled by qualified applicants, those seats are released to the General Public waitlist.

## üó≥Ô∏è The Selection Process
*Transparency is key. Here is what happens after you hit "Submit."*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Round 1: Blind Screen">
    **Who:** Program Manager.
    **Action:** We review your written application **without seeing your name or photo** (to reduce bias). We score strictly on the "Idea" and "Evidence".
    **Outcome:** Pass / Fail.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Round 2: The Interview">
    **Who:** Startup Success Lead + 1 Mentor.
    **Action:** A 20-30 minute Zoom call. This is where we score **Coachability** and **Resilience**.
    **Outcome:** Weighted Score (1-5).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Round 3: The Consensus Meeting">
    **Who:** Selection Committee (COO, Program Leads).
    **Action:** We review the leaderboard. We debate borderline cases and ensure the 70/30 quota balance is met.
    **Outcome:** Final Selection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
