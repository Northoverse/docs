# üó∫Ô∏è The Founder Journey
*Before you enter a specific track, most applicants go through this standard intake funnel to ensure they are matched with the right resources.*



[Image of application process flow chart]


<Steps>
  <Step title="Phase 1: Online Application">
    **Status:** Rolling Basis
    Submit the Intake Form on the specific program page.
    * **Requirement:** 200-word description of your idea & your primary goal.
    * **Quota Selection:** You must identify as either a **General Citizen** or **Student Graduate**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Phase 2: Screening & Eligibility">
    **Timeline:** 3‚Äì5 Business Days
    Our internal team reviews your submission against our [Eligibility Criteria](/eligibility).
    * **Focus:** We look for "Grit" and "Community Fit" over perfect business plans.
    * **Outcome:** Interview Invite, Deferral, or Referral to partner agency.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Phase 3: The 'Vibe Check' Interview">
    **Format:** 20-Minute Zoom Call
    A conversation to assess coachability and time commitment.
    * **For ETP:** We check willingness to learn.
    * **For Incubation:** We discuss business viability.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Phase 4: Acceptance & Onboarding">
    **Action:** Sign Agreements
    You receive access to the Northoverse HQ Slack, the Legal Aid portal, and the event calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>
