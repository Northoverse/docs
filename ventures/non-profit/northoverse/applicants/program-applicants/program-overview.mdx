# üöÄ The Program Catalog

Northoverse runs three distinct programming tracks annually. Unlike a university, we are an **NGO Incubator**. We exist to help citizens launch incorporated entities.

> **The 70/30 Split:**
> * **70% of Seats:** Open to the general public (Main Street founders, career switchers, citizens).
> * **30% of Seats:** Reserved exclusively for graduates of the **Northoverse College Network** (UPEI/Holland College).
