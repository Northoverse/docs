<Columns cols={1}>
  #### Humanity First

  <Card icon="1">
    We build companies that uplift people.
Every venture under Project1505 must create value that supports communities, improves lives, and expands opportunity for all—no matter where they live or what resources they have.
  </Card>
  #### Innovation With Purpose

  <Card icon="2">
    We innovate not for competition, but for contribution.
Our solutions must solve real problems, empower individuals and businesses, and create stepping stones toward humanity’s long-term technological advancement.
  </Card>
  #### Sustainability at Every Stage

  <Card icon="3">
    Growth must not come at the cost of our planet.
We prioritize responsible production, renewable systems, and efficient operations—ensuring that every chapter of our roadmap contributes to a sustainable future.
  </Card>
  #### Collaboration Over Isolation

  <Card icon="4">
    Each P1505 venture is interconnected.
We believe progress accelerates when teams, companies, and communities share knowledge, support one another, and move forward together.
  </Card>
  #### Accessibility & Opportunity

  <Card icon="5">
    We create tools, systems, and organizations that lower barriers.
From community incubation hubs to global remote work platforms, we aim to make entrepreneurship, technology, and employment accessible to everyone.
  </Card>
  #### Bold Exploration

  <Card icon="6">
    We embrace the unknown.
Our work is grounded on Earth, but our ambition reaches far beyond. Preparing humanity for advanced space travel requires courage, experimentation, and a willingness to challenge what seems impossible.
  </Card>
  #### Integrity & Responsibility

  <Card icon="7">
    We operate with transparency, honesty, and long-term commitment.
Our mission spans decades; credibility and trust are essential for sustaining a roadmap designed to outlive us.
  </Card>
  #### Future-Building Mindset

  <Card icon="8">
    Every company we create must lay bricks for the next.
From manufacturing and logistics to remote work and space research, each chapter adds to the foundation that will eventually carry humanity beyond our solar system.
  </Card>
  #### Community-Driven Growth

  <Card icon="9">
    The people we serve shape the direction we take.
We listen, adapt, and build ventures that respond to real human needs—empowering communities as partners, not customers.
  </Card>
</Columns>
